Project 3: Mutation Analysis for the gene SMCHD1

This project focuses on analyzing mutations in the SMCHD1 gene, which plays a key role in epigenetic regulation and has been implicated in diseases such as facioscapulohumeral muscular dystrophy (FSHD) and Bosma arhinia microphthalmia syndrome (BAMS). The goal is to identify and assess the potential functional impacts of mutations in SMCHD1 using various bioinformatics tools.
